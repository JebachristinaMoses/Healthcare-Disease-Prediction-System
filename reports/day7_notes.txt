Day 7: Correlation Analysis

Features Most Related to Diabetes:

1. Glucose (0.49)

2. BMI (0.31)

3. Age (0.24)

4. Pregnancies (0.22)

Observations:

1. Glucose has the highest correlation with diabetes (0.49), indicating that blood glucose level is the most important factor in predicting diabetes.

2. BMI shows a moderate positive correlation (0.31), suggesting that overweight and obesity increase diabetes risk.

3. Age and number of pregnancies are also positively correlated with diabetes, indicating that older individuals and women with more pregnancies have a higher likelihood of developing diabetes.

4. All features show positive correlations with the target variable, meaning they contribute to diabetes prediction to varying degrees.

5. Correlation analysis helps identify the most influential features that will be useful during machine learning model training.
